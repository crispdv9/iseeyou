Security cameras are everywhere and breaches in them are a rising issue. How would you feel knowing that someone unknown is watching your camera?

The project explores vulnerabilities from the misuse of security cameras. We took six different breached cameras and, for each, we unveil the process of discovering where they are placed and what or who they are surveilling.

Choose a camera, discover how we tracked them and see how people react.
